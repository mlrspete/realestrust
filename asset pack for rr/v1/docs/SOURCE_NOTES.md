# SOURCE_NOTES

## Intended final hero direction
A dark premium campaign-board hero assembled from a terrain/map anchor, restrained paper materials, physical board hardware, and a small set of Rust-native supporting cards that can animate into place with GSAP as independent layers.

## Why Rust-native cards were prioritized
The main quality issue in the previous direction was drift into generic stock imagery. The scenic and industrial cards need to feel unmistakably Rust-native so the board reads as a Real Rust activation board rather than a generic “strategy moodboard”.

## Why the previous generic-stock direction was rejected
Generic industrial photography or non-Rust landscapes flatten the concept and make the whole hero feel like an ad agency board for any category. The point of this pack is to preserve Rust-specific atmosphere while keeping the composition cinematic and premium.

## Substitutions made in this pass
- I created the production-safe board materials in-house rather than importing mixed-license stock fragments. That keeps the pack cohesive and removes downstream licensing friction.
- I created the map base in-house as a premium terrain poster instead of forcing a weaker utility-map style asset.
- I did **not** place Rust-native imagery in production because the legal position is nuanced for a commercial-facing use case. Instead I documented official candidates in review-required.

## Limitations encountered
- I could identify strong official Facepunch media candidates, but I could not confidently treat them as production-safe for this specific commercial use without a manual legal/brand review.
- I did not find a clean Rust-native cartographic inset that met the brief and had a clearly comfortable rights position.
- GitHub access in this session did not expose an obvious “Real Rust” destination repo, so the completed pack is delivered as a structured local artifact.

## Best 5 assets in the pack and why
1. `core/map-base-4k.png` — strongest anchor piece; it gives the hero immediate geography and negative space.
2. `board-elements/loose-leaf-note-01.png` — premium paper slab that can hold copy or sit as a tactile layer.

4. `board-elements/push-pin-red-01.png` — small accent asset that gives the composition intent and focus.

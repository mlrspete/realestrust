# Source Notes

## Overall visual direction
This pack was curated for a premium campaign-board / map-board hero: restrained, editorial, cinematic, Rust-adjacent in mood, and suitable for a dark high-end landing page once styled.

## Tradeoffs made
- A clearly rights-cleared Rust-specific clean terrain export was not sourced confidently enough under the usage-rights rules, so the map base uses a strong public-domain island/seafloor map instead.
- The optional image cards are not Rust-native screenshots. They were chosen because their rights were clear and their visual language fits the intended premium industrial / environmental location-card role better than uncertain Rust imagery.

## Asset substitutions
- `map-base-4k.png` is a public-domain island/seafloor terrain composition rather than a Rust map export.
- `grain-overlay.png` was derived from a CC0 4K noise texture to make it more directly useful as a transparent scene overlay.

## Processing kept deliberately light
- The map base was center-cropped and resized for hero composition.
- The grain texture was converted into a subtle transparent-ready overlay.
- The photo cards and paper/cardboard textures were not heavily edited.

## Optional photo-card outcome
Both optional photo cards were successfully sourced with acceptable rights:
- `photo-industrial-primary.jpg`
- `photo-environment-secondary.jpg`
